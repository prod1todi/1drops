What each license allows you to do with beats purchased on 1Drops

✓ Music videos (YouTube, social media)
✓ Free mixtapes and non-profit releases
✗ Not for commercial album sales

$100 for Pro members · $150 for Free members
✓ Non-exclusive use
✓ Unlimited streams / plays
✓ Commercial releases and album sales

$500 · Same price for all members

✓ Negotiable — contact Todi directly
Custom Beat License

All purchases are final. By purchasing a license you agree to these terms. For questions contact Todi directly through the platform.
